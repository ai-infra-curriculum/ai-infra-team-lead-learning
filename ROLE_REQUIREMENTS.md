# Role Requirements: AI Infrastructure Team Lead / Engineering Manager

**Level:** 3.5
**Experience Level:** 4-8 years in engineering with 1-3 years in leadership/management roles, or strong senior engineer transitioning to management
**Market Demand:** High - Strong demand for technical managers who can build and lead AI infrastructure teams. Companies need leaders who understand both technology and people. Particularly valued are those with hands-on AI infrastructure experience. Critical role as companies scale AI initiatives and build dedicated infrastructure teams.
**Salary Range (US, 2025):** $160,000 - $350,000 (median $210,000)

## Core Responsibilities
- Lead and manage team of 5-12 AI infrastructure engineers
- Recruit, hire, and build high-performing engineering teams
- Conduct performance reviews and career development planning
- Set team goals and priorities aligned with business objectives
- Manage team capacity and resource allocation
- Drive agile development processes and team workflows
- Provide technical guidance and unblock team challenges
- Collaborate with stakeholders on roadmap and requirements
- Manage team budget and infrastructure spending
- Foster team culture, collaboration, and psychological safety
- Facilitate cross-functional coordination with ML and product teams
- Drive continuous improvement in team processes
- Handle conflict resolution and team dynamics
- Report progress and metrics to senior leadership
- Balance technical innovation with delivery commitments

## Technical Skills
### Required
- Strong technical background in AI/ML infrastructure (hands-on in recent years)
- Understanding of ML lifecycle and infrastructure needs
- Knowledge of cloud platforms and infrastructure technologies
- Familiarity with modern ML frameworks and tools
- Understanding of DevOps and MLOps practices
- Ability to review technical designs and make sound decisions
- Knowledge of software development best practices
- Understanding of system architecture and scalability
- Technical credibility with engineering teams
- Staying current with AI infrastructure trends

### Preferred
- Recent hands-on experience as senior engineer
- Experience with GPU infrastructure and optimization
- Knowledge of distributed systems and ML at scale
- Understanding of emerging AI technologies
- Experience with technical roadmap planning
- Familiarity with infrastructure cost optimization
- Knowledge of security and compliance requirements
- Experience with technical debt management

## Soft Skills
- People management and empathy
- Effective communication (written and verbal)
- Active listening and feedback delivery
- Conflict resolution and mediation
- Strategic thinking and planning
- Stakeholder management and influence
- Coaching and mentoring
- Decision-making under uncertainty
- Emotional intelligence
- Building trust and psychological safety
- Time management and prioritization
- Adaptability and resilience

## Prerequisites
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field required; advanced degree beneficial
- Proven track record as senior engineer or tech lead
- Experience leading projects and mentoring engineers
- Strong technical foundation in AI/ML infrastructure
- Demonstrated people leadership potential or experience
- Understanding of agile methodologies
- Experience with cross-functional collaboration

## Day-to-Day Activities
- 1-on-1 meetings with team members
- Team planning and prioritization sessions
- Stakeholder meetings on roadmap and requirements
- Removing blockers and supporting team
- Reviewing technical designs and code (as needed)
- Facilitating retrospectives and process improvements
- Hiring activities (interviews, candidate reviews)
- Performance evaluations and career conversations
- Reporting status and metrics to leadership
- Budget planning and resource allocation
- Attending leadership and cross-team meetings
- Staying connected to technical work (limited hands-on)

## Success Metrics
- Team delivery on committed roadmap items (on-time, quality)
- Team member satisfaction and engagement scores
- Employee retention and team stability
- Team productivity and velocity trends
- Quality of deliverables and production incidents
- Hiring success (time to fill, offer acceptance rate)
- Career growth and development of team members
- Stakeholder satisfaction with team outcomes
- Infrastructure reliability and performance metrics
- Budget adherence and cost optimization
- Team collaboration and cross-functional effectiveness

## Common Challenges
- Transitioning from IC to management mindset
- Balancing hands-on technical work with people management
- Handling underperformance and difficult conversations
- Managing competing priorities and stakeholder demands
- Building and scaling team while maintaining culture
- Staying technically relevant while managing
- Dealing with team conflicts and dynamics
- Managing up to leadership and down to team
- Protecting team from organizational churn
- Balancing short-term delivery with long-term investments

## Key Technologies
- **Programming Languages:** Understanding of Python and infrastructure languages, Able to read and review code in team's languages
- **ML Frameworks:** Awareness of PyTorch, TensorFlow, and ML ecosystem, Understanding of team's ML infrastructure needs
- **Infrastructure:** Knowledge of cloud platforms and Kubernetes, Understanding of infrastructure at scale, Awareness of emerging infrastructure technologies
- **DevOps Tools:** Understanding of CI/CD and automation, Knowledge of infrastructure as code, Familiarity with DevOps best practices
- **MLOps Tools:** Awareness of MLOps platforms and tools, Understanding of ML workflow requirements
- **Monitoring & Observability:** Understanding of observability and monitoring, Ability to interpret infrastructure metrics
- **Data Processing:** Awareness of data pipeline technologies, Understanding of data infrastructure needs
- **GPU Tools:** Understanding of GPU infrastructure (if relevant to team), Awareness of GPU optimization concepts
- **Other Tools:** Project management tools (Jira, Asana, Linear), Communication tools (Slack, Teams), Documentation platforms (Confluence, Notion), Performance management systems, Hiring and recruiting platforms

## Recommended Certifications
- Leadership and management training (beneficial)
- Agile/Scrum certifications (CSM, SAFe)
- People management certifications
- Technical certifications maintained for credibility
- Executive education programs (beneficial for growth)
